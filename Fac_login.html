LOGIN AS FACULTY
This self-assessment for online teaching will allow you to evaluate and reflect upon your competencies in key areas of online teaching and provide a baseline of your pedagogical, technical, and administrative skills
  • The information you provide is used solely to send you the results of the self-assessment, and is not stored on the server
  • There are eighteen questions distributed in three categories within the assessment. The questions are based upon BMSCE's Faculty Competencies for Online Teaching All three categories must be completed in full
  • For each question, please select the response that best represents you
  • Once you submit all of your answers under a particular category you will be taken to another category
  • A copy of the report will be emailed to the address you provide when you begin
